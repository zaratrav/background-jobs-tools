About Background Jobs

Background jobs are essential for handling asynchronous tasks such as email sending, data processing, and scheduled operations.

Cron expressions allow precise scheduling, while job queues and worker concurrency ensure efficient task processing at scale.

Concepts like idempotency and retry strategies help build reliable systems that can handle failures gracefully.
